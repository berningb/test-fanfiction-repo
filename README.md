# Test Fanfiction Repository

This is a test repository for the FicFlow dashboard. It contains sample chapters with characters, locations, and relationships.

## Characters

- **Alex** - The protagonist, a young mage discovering their powers
- **Morgan** - Alex's mentor and friend
- **Luna** - A mysterious figure with hidden knowledge
- **River** - A warrior from the northern kingdoms
- **Sage** - The wise council member

## Locations

- **Eldoria** - The capital city
- **Shadowmere Forest** - A dark and dangerous woodland
- **Crystal Tower** - The ancient mage academy
- **Northern Keep** - River's homeland
