# Character Notes

## Alex
- **Role**: Protagonist, young mage
- **Powers**: Growing magical abilities, connection to nature
- **Relationships**: 
  - Close mentor relationship with Morgan
  - Mysterious connection with Luna
  - Growing friendship with River
- **Location**: Originally from a small village, now based in Eldoria
- **Arc**: Discovering their power and learning to use it responsibly

## Morgan
- **Role**: Mentor, experienced mage
- **Powers**: Skilled in defensive and barrier magic
- **Relationships**:
  - Mentor and friend to Alex
  - Respected by Sage and the council
  - Trusted ally of Luna
- **Location**: Based at Crystal Tower, travels frequently
- **Arc**: Guiding Alex while learning to let them grow independently

## Luna
- **Role**: Mysterious guide, keeper of secrets
- **Powers**: Shadow magic, knowledge of hidden paths
- **Relationships**:
  - Mysterious connection to Alex's destiny
  - Trusted by Morgan
  - Strategic partner to River
- **Location**: Unknown origin, appears when needed
- **Arc**: Revealing their true purpose and past

## River
- **Role**: Warrior, protector of Northern Keep
- **Powers**: Combat skills, leadership
- **Relationships**:
  - Growing friendship with Alex
  - Respectful partnership with Morgan
  - Strategic alliance with Luna
- **Location**: Northern Keep
- **Arc**: Balancing duty to their people with new alliances

## Sage
- **Role**: Wise council member, keeper of knowledge
- **Powers**: Knowledge and wisdom
- **Relationships**:
  - Advisor to all the main characters
  - Connection to Crystal Tower's history
- **Location**: Crystal Tower
- **Arc**: Providing guidance and historical context
