# Relationship Map

## Primary Relationships

### Alex ↔ Morgan
- **Type**: Mentor/Student, evolving into friendship
- **Strength**: Very Strong
- **Description**: Morgan guides Alex's growth while learning to trust in their student's abilities. The relationship deepens throughout the story.

### Alex ↔ Luna
- **Type**: Mysterious connection, destiny-linked
- **Strength**: Strong (growing)
- **Description**: There's something deeper connecting Alex and Luna that neither fully understands yet. The relationship hints at greater mysteries.

### Alex ↔ River
- **Type**: Friendship, mutual respect
- **Strength**: Strong
- **Description**: Alex and River bond over shared challenges and mutual respect. Their relationship represents the connection between different worlds.

### Morgan ↔ Luna
- **Type**: Trusted allies
- **Strength**: Strong
- **Description**: Morgan and Luna have worked together before, and their relationship is built on mutual respect and shared goals.

### River ↔ Luna
- **Type**: Strategic partnership
- **Strength**: Moderate (growing)
- **Description**: River and Luna work together strategically, and their relationship develops as they face challenges together.

## Secondary Relationships

### All Four (Alex, Morgan, Luna, River)
- **Type**: Found family, team
- **Strength**: Very Strong
- **Description**: The relationship between all four main characters creates a powerful bond that makes them stronger together.

### Sage ↔ All Characters
- **Type**: Advisor, mentor figure
- **Strength**: Moderate
- **Description**: Sage provides guidance and wisdom to all the main characters, representing the relationship between knowledge and action.
