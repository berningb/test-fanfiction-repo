# Location Guide

## Eldoria
The capital city, a bustling metropolis where the story begins and ends. Home to the palace and many important political figures. The relationship between Eldoria and the other locations is central to the story's political landscape.

## Shadowmere Forest
A dark and ancient woodland filled with magical energy. The forest has a deep connection to Alex's powers, and the relationship between the forest and magic users is complex. Many important events take place here.

## Crystal Tower
The ancient mage academy, a place of learning and power. The tower stands as a symbol of knowledge and the relationship between different magical traditions. Sage and many other mages call it home.

## Northern Keep
River's homeland, a fortress built into a mountain. The keep represents strength and resilience, and the relationship between the northern people and the rest of the kingdom is important to the story's political dynamics.
